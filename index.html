Helen Blewett | Practice Lead - Analytics

The future of data and analytics will be different from what we have today — agentic AI promises to move analytics from reactive tools to proactive, goal-driven assistants that can reason, plan and act across enterprise data.

The evolution of Qlik AI

Over the last several years Qlik has evolved its analytics engine and introduced a range of AI capabilities — from natural language assistants to predictive tools — positioning the platform to power agent-based, actionable analytics across structured and unstructured data.

Example Embedded Visual

Where this is headed

Agentic AI will combine structured analytics with unstructured knowledge sources, letting agents reason across both to produce the most relevant recommendations and, when authorised, execute actions across systems.

As the platform and ecosystem evolve, the emphasis will be on interoperability, governance and the ability to embed purpose-built assistants into business workflows.
